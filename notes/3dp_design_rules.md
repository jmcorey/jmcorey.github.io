# Design rules

## FDM/FFF

### Walls

Min 0.8mm thick.

### Overhangs

Max 45 degrees.

### Raised or recessed feature size

Min 0.6mm wide and 2mm high.

### Horizontal bridges

Max 10mm w/o support.

### Holes

Min 2mm diameter.

### Moving parts clearance

Min 0.5mm.

### Pins

Min 3mm diameter.
